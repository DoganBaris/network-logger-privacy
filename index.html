Privacy Policy for Network & Console Logger

Last updated: November 25, 2025 (25 Kasım 2025)

1. Data Collection

Network & Console Logger does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. All network and console logs captured by the extension remain entirely on the user's local device.

The extension has no backend server, and no data is ever sent outside your machine.

2. Permissions Used

The extension requires the following permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

  • webRequest – to capture and display network requests in real time
  • tabs – to associate logs with the correct browser tab
  • storage – to store extension settings locally
  • downloads – to export logs as files on the user’s device
  • <all_urls> – to enable logging on any website the user chooses

These permissions are used only to provide logging functionality and never to collect personal data.

3. Local-Only Operation

All operations happen entirely within the browser. No data is uploaded, synchronized, or transmitted to any external service.
